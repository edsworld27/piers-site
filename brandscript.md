# StoryBrand BrandScript: Piers Day Hypnotherapy

*Based on Donald Miller's Building a StoryBrand Framework*

This document is a living framework to ensure all copywriting and design decisions constantly position the customer as the Hero and Piers Day as the Guide. 

---

## 1. The Character (The Hero)
*The customer is the hero of the story, not the brand. What do they want?*

**Who are they:** People struggling with anxiety, addiction, or limiting beliefs.
**What do they want:** To take back control of their mind and improve their lives. They want relief from the constant, exhausting fight against themselves.

## 2. Has a Problem
*The hook that makes them care. We must agitate the problem.*

**Villain:** The primitive "survival brain" (The Subconscious).
**External Problem:** Crippling anxiety, smoking/addictions, weight management issues, performance anxiety.
**Internal Problem:** "I feel broken. I feel weak. Why can't I just willpower my way through this? I'm exhausted."
**Philosophical Problem:** You shouldn't have to fight your own mind every day to live a normal life. You deserve to thrive, not just survive.

## 3. And Meets a Guide
*Customers aren't looking for another hero; they are looking for a guide.*

**Empathy:** *"We know exactly how heavy and exhausting it feels to try and fix this on your own. It's terrifying when you feel out of control."*
**Authority/Competency:** *"As clinical hypnotherapists with 25+ years of experience, we have helped hundreds of people solve this exact problem by rewiring the root cause."*

## 4. Who Gives Them a Plan
*We must clear the fog. The plan removes the risk of doing business with us.*

*   **Step 1. Reach Out Your Way:** Fill out a quick 3-question form, send an email, or just call us directly. Zero pressure.
*   **Step 2. Free Intake Review:** We figure out exactly what your problem is, why you are stuck, and how we can solve it for you.
*   **Step 3. Get Your Plan & Tools:** You'll walk away with a clear plan and the mental equipment to support you along the way so you can finally thrive.

## 5. And Calls Them to Action
*Customers do not take action unless they are challenged to take action.*

**Direct CTA:** "Get Support Now" / "Let's Talk" / "Reach Out Today"
**Transitional CTA:** (e.g., Download a free guide on "Understanding Your Survival Brain") - *To be developed*

## 6. That Helps Them Avoid Failure (The Stakes)
*What terrible thing will happen if they don't do business with us? We must agitate the stakes.*

**The Failure:** "I have seen far too many people stop their lives and fail to reach their potential because they let their primal fears dictate their future. They lose time, they damage their health, and they accept a life of just 'surviving'. Don't waste another day."

## 7. And Ends in a Success
*What will their life look like when their problem is solved?*

**The Success:** You take back control. The constant "fight or flight" noise stops. You sleep deeply, perform at your highest potential, add years to your life, and finally experience what it feels like to thrive instead of just survive.

---

### Overarching One-Liner (The "One Thing")
"We give you back control of your mind by erasing limiting beliefs. We do this one thing incredibly well, and apply it in hundreds of ways to improve your life."
